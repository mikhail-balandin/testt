Digital signage system for centralised management of displays of informational, social and promotional content in public places

About project

The system consists of a web application and a hardware player connected to the screen. The player receives content and playback instructions over the network.

The system allows you to configure a schedule of shows, a crawl line. It supports files of various formats, including video up to 4K resolution, images, PDF documents, web pages. Can broadcast live on a dynamic website, including all standard video hosters, media players and online TV services.

The player can work offline without being connected to the network; playback files are stored locally on the device and downloaded once, without burdening the network.
